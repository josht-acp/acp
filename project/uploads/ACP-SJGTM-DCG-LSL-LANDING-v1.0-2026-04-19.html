The Strategic Case — One Paragraph

Each party brings a structurally distinct, non-substitutable capability. ACP alone has pipeline without committed capital velocity. DCG alone has allocation without structured origination. Lendsecure alone has credit expertise without proprietary pipeline. The alliance resolves all three simultaneously — and does so under a single buy-box, a single governance protocol and a single economic framework. The platform is designed to be programmatic, scalable and replicable.

Economic Guardrails — Non-Negotiable

No cross-subsidy

Every mandate stands alone economically. No bundling, no blended pricing across unrelated transactions.

MFN across alliance

No counterparty is offered better terms than ACP on the same mandate. Most-favoured-nation applies across all economics.

24-month tail

Full success fee on any transaction with introduced parties for 24 months post-termination. Standard ACP tail protection.

10-day upstream settlement

Fee invoicing on ACP paper; upstream settlement to DCG and Lendsecure within 10 business days of receipt.
